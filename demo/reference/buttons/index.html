Buttons

Buttons can be tricky little buggers to get right. With the exception of the simple Primary and Secondary styles, buttons will almost always be styled depending on the project and can quickly become unwieldy.

Some projects demand complex button combinations while others may not demand more than the standard set. The trickiness comes when dealing with a button’s background colour, hover state colour, text colour (hover and normal states) and the colour of whatever the background is behind the button.

The best course of action (and this is really for the designers) is to start with and try to keep things simple. Don’t combine too many combinations and be wary of where buttons are used.
